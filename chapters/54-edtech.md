# Chapter 54: EdTech & Learning

**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

---

## Overview

Educational technology creates digital learning experiences that transform how people acquire knowledge and skills. From learning management systems to adaptive tutoring, EdTech combines pedagogy with technology.

This chapter covers essential skills for building effective, engaging learning platforms.

### Skills Covered in This Chapter

| Skill | Purpose | Best For |
|-------|---------|----------|
| `@edtech-architect` | Learning platforms | LMS, course platforms |
| `@learning-designer` | Learning design | Course structure, pedagogy |
| `@assessment-expert` | Assessment systems | Testing, grading |
| `@adaptive-learning` | Personalized learning | AI-driven adaptation |

## Best Practices Summary

### Platform Design
1. **Engagement First:** Learning must be engaging
2. **Mobile Ready:** Learning anywhere
3. **Offline Support:** Unreliable connections
4. **Accessibility:** Universal design
5. **Performance:** Video must stream smoothly

### Learning Design
1. **Clear Outcomes:** Measurable objectives
2. **Active Learning:** Do, don't just watch
3. **Spaced Practice:** Reinforce over time
4. **Feedback:** Immediate and actionable
5. **Social:** Community enhances learning

### Assessment
1. **Alignment:** Assess what you teach
2. **Fairness:** Equal opportunity
3. **Validity:** Measure what you intend
4. **Reliability:** Consistent results
5. **Security:** Prevent cheating
