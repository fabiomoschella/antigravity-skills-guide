# Chapter 47: Productivity & Time Management

**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

## Overview

Developer productivity is about working smarter, not just harder. Effective time management, workflow automation, and tool mastery enable engineers to focus on high-value work while minimizing friction and context switching.

This chapter covers essential skills for maximizing individual and team productivity in software development.

### Skills Covered in This Chapter

| Skill | Purpose | Best For |
|-------|---------|----------|
| `@productivity-coach` | Personal productivity | Time and energy management |
| `@automation-expert` | Workflow automation | Eliminating repetitive tasks |

| `@focus-coach` | Deep work practices | Concentration and flow |

**Context:** You want to restructure your workday for maximum productivity.


## Best Practices Summary

### Time Management
1. **Time Blocking:** Protect focused time
2. **Batching:** Group similar tasks
3. **Energy Mapping:** Match tasks to energy
4. **Weekly Review:** Regular planning
5. **Buffer Time:** Leave margin

### Automation
1. **Measure First:** Quantify time spent
2. **Start Small:** Quick wins build momentum
3. **Document:** Make automations maintainable
4. **Share:** Team-wide benefits
5. **Iterate:** Continuous improvement

### Focus
1. **Rituals:** Consistent start routines
2. **Environment:** Design for focus
3. **Boundaries:** Protect focus time
4. **Single-Tasking:** One thing at a time
5. **Recovery:** Rest enables focus
